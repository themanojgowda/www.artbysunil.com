ABOUT

I’m an Art Director based in India with over a decade of experience in the art department for film and television. My background in Graphic Design laid the foundation for my journey from creating visual concepts to leading creative teams and designing immersive cinematic environments.

My work spans everything from large-scale film sets to intricate design details, where I specialize in turning abstract ideas into tangible, captivating spaces. I combine practical execution with visionary creativity to build environments that elevate storytelling.

I’ve had the privilege of collaborating with some of the industry’s most respected professionals, including National Award Winner Vinesh Banglan, T.P. Abid, Priya Suhass, T. Santhanam, and Sanjay Dasgupta. I’m known for my calm leadership and grounded approach, fostering collaborative teams that thrive on clarity and cultural relevance.

I’m passionate about creating visually rich worlds that bring narratives to life, balancing structure and imagination, guided by strong aesthetics and cultural context.
